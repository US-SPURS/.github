# Welcome to US-SPURS on GitHub

## United States Department of Special Projects and Unified Response Services

The United States Department of Special Projects and Unified Response Services (US-SPURS) is an Independent Federal Agency (IFA) committed to transparency, innovation, and collaboration through open source development.

### 🏢 About Us

Located at **Joint Force Base Freedom Island** (formerly Alameda Naval Air Station) in Alameda, California, US-SPURS coordinates specialized projects and provides unified response capabilities across federal agencies.

### 🎯 Our Mission

We bring together expertise from across the federal government to:

- 🚀 Coordinate specialized cross-agency projects
- ⚡ Provide rapid unified response to emerging challenges
- 💡 Develop innovative solutions to complex problems
- 🤝 Foster collaboration at all government levels
- 🔓 Maintain transparency through open source development

### 🌟 Open Source Commitment

We believe in the power of open collaboration. Our open source projects demonstrate our commitment to:

- **Transparency** - Making our work visible to the public
- **Collaboration** - Working with developers, agencies, and citizens
- **Innovation** - Leveraging community expertise and ideas
- **Efficiency** - Sharing solutions to avoid duplicated effort
- **Security** - Benefiting from community review and testing

### 🔐 Security

Security is paramount. If you discover a vulnerability:

- **DO NOT** open a public issue
- **DO** email security@spurs.gov
- See our [Security Policy](https://github.com/US-SPURS/.github/blob/main/SECURITY.md) for details
